Guessing Game

-designed by Andrew Scheuermann

This is a guessing game in which you have 6 chances to guess the right number.
Hints will cost you two guesses!
With each guess you will be told if you are "too high" or "too low" and how hot or cold you are....
-COLD: 25 or more numbers away
-LUKEWARM: 12 - 24 numbers away
-HOT: 6 - 11 numbers away
-BOILING: 1 - 5 numbers away
